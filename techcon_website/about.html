History:

The history of tech events dates back to the early days of the computer industry. One of the earliest and most iconic tech events is the launch of the original Macintosh computer by Apple in 1984. This event, which featured the famous "1984" commercial directed by Ridley Scott, is remembered as a watershed moment in the history of personal computing.

Since then, tech events have evolved to become major showcases for new products, innovations, and industry trends. Events like the Consumer Electronics Show (CES) and the Apple Worldwide Developers Conference (WWDC) have become key platforms for companies to unveil their latest gadgets and software developments.

These events play a crucial role in shaping the tech industry, driving consumer excitement, and setting the stage for the next wave of technological advancements. From the unveiling of groundbreaking products to the introduction of revolutionary technologies, tech events continue to capture the imagination of enthusiasts and professionals alike.

TechCon, which has been running for a decade now, is no different from the aforementioned. It gives budding innovators the opportunity to showcase their projects and inventions. TechCon is known worldwide and brings people all around the world together to witness greatness in the continent of Africa.

Mission:

TechCon's mission is to bring together technology enthusiasts from around the world to share knowledge, collaborate on innovative ideas, and inspire the next generation of technological advancements. With a decade-long history of fostering connections and driving progress in the tech industry, TechCon is committed to providing a platform for learning, networking, and exploring the latest trends and developments. By uniting industry professionals, thought leaders, and aspiring individuals, TechCon aims to cultivate a vibrant community that propels technology forward and empowers individuals to turn their visions into reality.

Past Speakers:

TechCon has had its fair share of powerful and moving public speakers, and we make it a priority to always invite these encouraging set of people, to motivate and empower the youths to push on in all their endeavors
